Hi thereüëã! This is JC, a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor with Department of Computing ([COMP](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/comp/people/academic-staff/prof-guo-jingcai/)) at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/en/), leading the [***<font color="Crimson">Lumen Lab</font>***](https://lumen-lab-polyu.github.io/) and, by courtesy, with Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics ([LSGI](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/lsgi/people/academic-staff/prof-jingcai-guo/)). I defended my Ph.D. in December 2020 at the same university and was fortunate to be supported by the [Hong Kong PhD Fellowship](https://cerg1.ugc.edu.hk/hkpfs/index.html) (**HKPFS**) from **Hong Kong RGC** during this period. From 2021 to 2022, I did postdoctoral training at [The University of Sydney](https://www.sydney.edu.au/), Australia, where I was also a Visiting Ph.D. from 2019 to 2020. Prior to that, I received my M.Phil. from [Waseda University](https://www.waseda.jp/top/), Japan, and B.Eng. from [Sichuan University](https://en.scu.edu.cn/), China, all in Computer Science and AI/ML. Earlier before I did my Ph.D., I spent nearly 2 years in the investment banking industry after Waseda. 

I am generally interested in ***<font color="Crimson">Efficient</font>*** and ***<font color="Crimson">Edge-Native</font>*** ***<font color="Crimson">AI</font>***, which targets learning/modeling with ***<font color="Crimson">Limited Resources</font>*** in terms of ***<font color="Crimson">Data</font>***, ***<font color="Crimson">Computing Capability</font>***, as well as exploring their derivative ***<font color="Crimson">Applications</font>*** on a wide spectrum, in particular at the edges. Some specific topics include **Zero/Few-shot Learning**, **Collaborative/Federated Learning**, **Representation Learning**, **Model Compression**, and **Lightweight Solutions** for **Foundation/Large Models**. Our works are published in various prestigious venues, including AAAI, IJCAI, CVPR, ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, MM, ATC, ICDCS, TNNLS, TPAMI, TMM, TC, TCSS, etc. As the **Principal Investigator (PI)**, I have secured multiple external research grants, including **General Research Funds (GRF)** from **Hong Kong RGC**, **Young Scientists Fund** from **NSFC**, **Industrial Funds**, etc. I have served as **Chair Members** for multiple leading CS conference, like Publicity Chair for SRDS 2022 (Vienna, Austria), Track Chair (TPC Vice-Chair) for VTC 2024 Fall (Washington DC, USA), Session Coordinator for ACM-MM 2025 (Dublin, Ireland). I have regularly served as **Area Chair** and/or **Senior PC** for prestigious venues like ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, ACM-MM, AAAI, IJCAI, etc. I am currently serving as **Executive Editor-in-Chief** for [*Edge Intelligence and Systems*](https://www.sciltp.com/journals/eis/editorial-board), serving as **Associate Editor** for [*Pattern Recognition*](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/pattern-recognition/about/editorial-board) and [*IEEE OJ-CS*](https://www.computer.org/csdl/journal/oj/about/107402?title=Editorial%20Board&periodical=IEEE%20Open%20Journal%20of%20the%20Computer%20Society), and serving as **Guest Editor** for [*IEEE TCSS*](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/RecentIssue.jsp?punumber=6570650) and [*Complex & Intelligent Systems*](https://link.springer.com/journal/40747). I am a member of IEEE and CCF.
